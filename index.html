Mission & Vision

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where: Learners learn in a child-friendly, safe, and motivating environment.
